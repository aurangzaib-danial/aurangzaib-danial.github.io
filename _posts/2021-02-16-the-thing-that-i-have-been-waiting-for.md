Hi everyone! I wanted to share the news that I have finally got my **car**. It's a **Honda Jazz** with **automatic transmission**. As everyone knew, I had been working really hard since last month to secure a car here in **Scotland**. The formalities that you need to fulfill apart from buying the car are:

1. Car insurance
2. MOT of car
3. Pay road tax of car

> Above procedures are recurring every year.

Anyways, it was hard to get everything done but it has been rewarding. I'm so much happy that in a short period of time after coming to the **UK**, I have been able to purchase a car.

## Traffic Rules
Driving here is easier than in **Pakistan** because everyone strictly obeys rules. However, there are a bunch of rules that you have to be careful about and for someone from **Asia**, it might be overwhelmingly but just be confident and try to learn the rules before driving here. I have gotten better understanding of the rules here but I have been taking my brother with me and everyday I learn new things from him which is a great opportunity.
