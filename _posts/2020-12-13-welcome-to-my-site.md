Hey!!!! Welcome everyone. This is my first post on my first ever personal website. I have made this blog using [Jekyll][jekyll], the popular blog engine for programmers. 

Jekyll is pretty simple because it's just static site generator. Whereas, wordpress etc. involve a lot of overhead and the best part of Jekyll is that I can write in markdown with **code blocks** highlighted. 

Further, I have really given writing on popular blog websites like [medium][medium] and [dev.to][dev.to] because they also involved a lot of overhead and I just did not feel comfortable on them. This Jekyll powered website is much more easier for me to manage and inspires creativity.

On my website's blog, I'm not going to be just writing about programming. I want to share about my thoughts on life, fun and just about everything. I want to share latest developments in my favorite **Japanese anime** and what I really love about them.

Well, that's about it for my welcome post and in my next post, I'm probably going to write about something fun...
